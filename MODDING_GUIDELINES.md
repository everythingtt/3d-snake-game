# Theme Customization Guidelines

To maintain a safe and legal environment for all players, all custom themes for **3D Snake Game - Space Journey** must adhere to the following guidelines.

## 1. Prohibited Content
You are strictly prohibited from creating, distributing, or promoting themes that contain:
- **Illegal Content**: Any content that violates local, national, or international laws.
- **NSFW/Adult Content**: Explicit sexual content, nudity, or highly suggestive material.
- **CASM (Child Abuse Sexual Material)**: We have a zero-tolerance policy for any material depicting or promoting child abuse or exploitation. Any such content will be reported to the appropriate authorities.
- **Hate Speech & Harassment**: Content that promotes discrimination, disparagement, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, identity, or orientation.
- **Executable Code**: Themes must be strictly data-driven (JSON, images, models). Any attempt to include or execute scripts or malicious code is strictly prohibited.

## 2. Intellectual Property
- Respect the copyrights and trademarks of others. Do not use assets (models, textures, music) that you do not have the right to use.
- Do not use logos, branding, or names that suggest false affiliation with or endorsement by third-party organizations (e.g., GitHub, Google, etc.).

## 3. Reporting Violations
If you encounter a theme that violates these guidelines, please remove it from your local installation immediately. As this is a client-side customization system, you are responsible for the content you choose to load.

## 4. Enforcement
Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in your themes being excluded from community listings or further legal action where applicable.
